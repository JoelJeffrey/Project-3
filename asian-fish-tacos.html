asian fish tacos

Prep: 15 min Total: 35 min

Makes 6 servings

you will need:

  • 12 corn tortillas (6 inch), warmed
  • 1/2 cup PLANTERS Dry Roasted Peanuts, chopped
  • 1 pkg. (16 oz) coleslaw blend (cabbage slaw mix)
  • 1-1/2 cups panko bread crumbs
  • 1-1/2 lb. tilapia fillets, cut crosswise into 1-inch-wide strips
  • 1 pkg. (12.2 oz.) KRAFT RECIPE MAKERS Asian Fish tacos

directions:

  1. HEAT oven to 375Â°F. Spray baking sheet with cooking spray. Dip fish in Baking Sauce and then in crumbs, turning to evenly coat both sides of each strip. Place on prepared baking sheet.
  2. BAKE 15 to 20 min. or until fish flakes easily with fork.
  3. MIX coleslaw blend, nuts and Finishing Sauce.
  4. TOP warm tortillas with fish and slaw mixture.

kitchen tips:

  • VARIATION Add a few drops of Sriracha sauce (hot chili sauce) to slaw; sprinkle with sliced green onions.
  • SUBSTITUTE Prepare sing your favorite firm white fish.
